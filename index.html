🎓 New Hire Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team! This guide will help you get started with your first 30 days at Block Learning.

💡
Pro Tip: Complete all Week 1 modules before attending your first live training session. This will help you get the most out of the interactive portions.

📅 30-Day Onboarding Timeline

Week 1: Fundamentals

  • Day 1-2: New Hire Orientation & System Access Setup
    Complete HR onboarding, set up accounts, meet your team
  • Day 3-4: SOP Overview & IDV Fundamentals
    Read compliance SOPs, complete IDV training module
  • Day 5: TIN Verification Workshop
    Live training session on TIN validation procedures

Week 2: Core Skills

  • Day 6-8: Bank Statement Review & Document Verification
    Learn to validate bank statements and authenticate documents
  • Day 9-10: Adverse Actions & SMLLC Procedures
    Master decline procedures and single-member LLC handling

Week 3: Advanced Topics

  • Day 11-15: Credit Risk Assessment & Manual Review
    Shadow experienced agents, review real cases with supervision

Week 4: Certification

  • Day 16-20: Practice Cases & Scenario Training
    Complete practice scenarios, take quizzes, review feedback
  • Day 21-30: Final Assessment & Certification
    Complete certification exam, begin handling live cases with oversight
