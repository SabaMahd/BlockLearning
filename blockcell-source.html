👋 Welcome!

Welcome to the SFS Pre-Origination Training Dashboard—your central hub for training schedules, resources, and skill-building! Track your progress, access SOPs and training materials, and sharpen your decision-making with our interactive simulators.

🤝 Who We Are

The SFS Pre-Origination Team reviews loan applications that require manual decisioning, helping provide Sellers with access to capital when applications are not auto-approved. The team continuously refines its review processes to balance effective risk management while maintaining a strong Seller experience.
